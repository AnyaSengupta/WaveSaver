Wave Saver Device

The Wave Saver Device updates our website and map for dangerous waves and tide levels.The Wave Saver is compact but extremely strong to fight current weather conditions. Our cutting edge technology features sensors such as a barometer, motion sensor, and pressure sensor.


More

This model was created on Autodesk Inventor and includes the basic features that we wanted our product to have; an extendable rod and a button that triggers it.

The two images above display the mechanism that is inside the handle that allows it to extend and retract. The image shows the hand when it is extended. This only shows the first two segments of the product in order to clearly display how the product works. The large image demonstrates a 3D model completed through Inventor Pro that shows our final product fully extended.
